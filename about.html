About Us

The story behind South London's most exciting R&B/Neo-Soul band!

Our Story

Any Kinda People was born in September 2023 when five exceptionally talented musicians from across South London discovered a shared musical chemistry that couldn't be ignored. Each member brings a distinct musical heritage to the ensemble. While their backgrounds are wonderfully diverse, they found common ground in their deep appreciation for authentic R&B and innovative Neo-Soul.

The band's name serves as both a statement and an invitation. "Any Kinda People" embodies their fundamental belief that music should be inclusive, boundary-free, and accessible to everyone regardless of background, age, or musical preference.

In just a short time, Any Kinda People has already begun making waves in South London's vibrant music scene, earning recognition for their authentic sound, dynamic stage presence, and genuine connection with audiences who appreciate music that comes straight from the heart.

Our Sound

Any Kinda People crafts a signature sound built around seamless vocal harmonies, intricate guitar melodies, and rhythm sections that create an undeniable groove. The band honors the rich legacy of classic soul while infusing it with contemporary production that feels both fresh and timeless.

Drawing from the soulful authenticity of "Erykah Badu", the innovative grooves of "D'Angelo", and the boundary-pushing approach of "Hiatus Kaiyote", the band has developed a distinctive musical identity. Their music explore universal themes of connection, personal growth, and community, creating songs that resonate with a wide audience while maintaining artistic depth.

On stage, Any Kinda People delivers dynamic performances characterized by authentic energy and strong audience engagement. Their live shows create immersive experiences that transform venues into shared spaces of musical connection, leaving a lasting impression that extends well beyond the final note.
