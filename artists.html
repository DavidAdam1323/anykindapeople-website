Meet The Band

"Get to know the talented musicians behind Any Kinda People."

David

Electric & Acoustic Guitar

As Any Kinda People's lead guitarist, David brings over two decades of musical expertise, crafting the intricate melodies and soulful solos that define the band's core sound. His guitar work provides both the emotional heart and structural foundation of their music.

Originally from Brazil, His distinctive style represents a mix of classic R&B and modern neo-soul, creating a sound that bridges generations while feeling entirely contemporary.

David's musical intuition shines through in his impeccable sense of timing and dynamics; knowing precisely when to command attention with a powerful solo or when to support the ensemble with subtle, textured rhythms. This thoughtful approach to performance makes him not just a skilled guitarist, but a genuine musical storyteller.

Musical Influences

Jimi Hendrix Prince Tom Misch Isaiah Sharkey

Pedro

Electric Bass Guitar

Pedro anchors Any Kinda People's rhythm section with his groove-oriented bass lines, providing the foundational pulse that drives the band's sound. His playing seamlessly bridges rhythmic precision with melodic sensibility, creating bass parts that are both supportive and inventive.

Originally from Costa Rica, Pedro's Latin American heritage infuses his playing with distinctive rhythmic sensibilities that complement the band's R&B and neo-soul foundation.

When not holding down the low end, Pedro pursues an accomplished academic career with advanced international studies in Japan focused on public policy and sustainable development. This intellectual curiosity and global perspective directly inform his musical approach, where he brings thoughtful composition and nuanced awareness to every performance.

Musical Influences

Thundercat Bootsy Collins Meshell Ndegeocello Larry Graham

Naima

Lead Vocals

Known as Insert Muse, Naima's powerful yet nuanced vocals serve as the emotional heart of Any Kinda People, delivering performances that connect deeply with audiences. Her voice possesses a rare ability to convey raw emotion while maintaining exceptional technical precision and control.

Emerging from South London's vibrant music scene, her distinctive vocal style masterfully blends the raw passion of classic soul with the smooth sophistication of contemporary R&B. This compelling fusion creates a sound that feels both timeless and refreshingly modern.

As Insert Muse, she skillfully weaves together personal experiences with universal truths, creating authentic musical narratives that resonate deeply with listeners. Her dynamic vocal range and artistic intuition make each performance an unforgettable journey.

Musical Influences

Erykah Badu Jill Scott Lianne La Havas Aretha Franklin

Christopher

Piano & Synthesizer Keys

Chris provides the harmonic foundation and textural depth that defines Any Kinda People's signature sound through his masterful piano and keyboard work. His versatile playing spans from ethereal synth pads to soulful Rhodes passages, creating the atmospheric backdrop for the band's musical narratives.

Hailing from India, Chris's musical journey includes extensive work with function bands and regular performances in worship settings. These diverse experiences have cultivated his sophisticated harmonic sensibility and innate understanding of how to serve each song's emotional core.

Chris's approach balances technical precision with heartfelt expression, whether providing subtle harmonic support or delivering memorable melodic phrases. His church background brings a spiritual dimension to his playing, adding depth and authenticity to the band's soulful R&B and neo-soul repertoire.

Musical Influences

Robert Glasper Herbie Hancock Nicolas Britell Oscar Peterson

Gareth

Drums & Rhythm

Gareth provides the rhythmic foundation and infectious grooves that drive Any Kinda People's sound forward with precision and soul. As a South London native and original soul-boy drummer, he brings authentic feel and timeless rhythmic sensibilities to the band's musical foundation.

Drawing from a rich tapestry of influences including classic soul grooves, sophisticated acid jazz, and recent urban samba experiences, Gareth creates a unique rhythmic blend that is both danceable and musically sophisticated. This diverse background allows him to navigate various styles with natural fluency.

His drumming serves as the heartbeat of the ensemble, knowing exactly when to provide solid backbone and when to add creative flourishes that elevate each composition. Gareth's intuitive sense of rhythm and deep musical knowledge make him the essential pulse that keeps the band connected and moving as one cohesive unit.

Musical Influences

Questlove Chris Dave Steve Gadd J Dilla
